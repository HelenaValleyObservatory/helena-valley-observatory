We are a way for the cosmos to know itself.

Tonight, from this small world in Montana,
we bear witness to:

  • Artificial satellites, humanity's first steps into the cosmos
  • Aircraft, our species learning to fly
  • The ancient light of distant suns

Each observation connects us to the universe.
Each measurement is an act of cosmic participation.

We are made of star-stuff,
observing star-stuff,
in a universe made entirely of star-stuff.
